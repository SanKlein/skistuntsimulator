How to play

Move the mouse to pose the skier — it's relative to the centre of the screen. Down = crouch/tuck, up = stand tall & extend, right = lean forward (front flip), left = lean back (backflip). Crouch then snap up fast to jump off the snow anywhere. Tuck to spin faster, extend and center to land upright. Press R to replay.

A tribute to the original Ski Stunt Simulator by Michiel van de Panne (UBC).
